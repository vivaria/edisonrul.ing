### Card Rulings

*   "Blast Juggler" targets 2 face-up monsters with 1000 or less ATK.
*   "Blast Juggler"'s effect cannot be activated if there is only 1 face-up monster (excluding "Blast Juggler") with 1000 or less ATK.
