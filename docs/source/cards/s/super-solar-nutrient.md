# Super Solar Nutrient

## Edisonformat.net (Revised, Post-UTW Rulings)

Source: [https://edisonformat.net/card?name=Super%20Solar%20Nutrient](https://edisonformat.net/card?name=Super%20Solar%20Nutrient)

### Edison-Accurate PSCT

> ① Activate this card (from your hand) by Tributing 1 Level 2 or lower Plant monster; Special Summon 1 Plant monster from your hand/Deck, whose Level is less than or equal to the Level the Tributed monster had on the field +3.
> ② When this card leaves the field, destroy that monster.
> ③ When that monster leaves the field, destroy this card.

### Card Rulings

The ①:
*   Tributing a Plant monster is a cost, which must be paid in order to activate this card from your hand.
*   This card can only ever summon 1 monster, similar to "Call of the Haunted".

The ② and ③:
*   This card continuously targets the card it summoned, similar to "Call of the Haunted"


## Edisonformat.com (Historical, Pre-UTW Rulings)

Source: [https://www.edisonformat.com/rulings](https://www.edisonformat.com/rulings)

### Card Text

> No card text found for this card.

### Card Rulings

*   Calculate the maximum Level of monster you can Special Summon based on the Level of the Tributed monster when it was on the field.
*   “Super Solar Nutrient” does not target a monster in your hand or Deck.
*   However, it does target the Special Summoned monster after it is Special Summoned.
*   Tributing a Plant for this card’s activation is a cost.
