# Guardian Tryce

## Edisonformat.net (Revised, Post-UTW Rulings)

Source: [https://edisonformat.net/card?name=Guardian%20Tryce](https://edisonformat.net/card?name=Guardian%20Tryce)

### Edison-Accurate PSCT

> No Edison-Accurate PSCT available for this card.

### Card Rulings

*   If you Special Summon Guardian Tryce, you don't get to activate his effect when he's destroyed, even if you Tribute Summoned him previously and are using Monster Reborn on him later.
*   You must Special Summon the exact same card that was Tributed for Guardian Tryce when using his effect; you can't Special Summon a card of the same name.
*   You can't use Guardian Tryce's effect to Special Summon a Special Summon-only monster that had not been properly Summoned, such as a Fusion Monster that was Special Summoned with Magical Scientist and then Tributed for Guardian Tryce.
*   You can't Special Summon Dark Ruler Ha Des with Guardian Tryce's effect.
*   You can Special Summon a monster in your opponent's GY if it was used to Tribute Summon Guardian Tryce.
*   If Guardian Tryce is an Equip Spell Card equipped to Relinquished or Sword Hunter, and Guardian Tryce is destroyed and sent to the GY, his effect activates.
*   If Guardian Tryce is Set and is destroyed, his effect activates.
